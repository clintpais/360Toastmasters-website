360° Toastmasters Club Bengaluru — One-page Website

How to use:
1) Upload this folder (or zip) to Netlify by drag-and-drop.
2) Update content in index.html as needed.

Logo note:
- club-logo.png is included.
- For Toastmasters International logo, please download the official asset from Toastmasters Brand Portal and place it in assets/tmi-logo.png,
  then uncomment the relevant section in index.html if you want to display it.


Gallery setup:
- Add your meeting photos into /assets as gallery-01.jpg ... gallery-06.jpg
- You may also add the official Toastmasters International logo as assets/tmi-logo.png (download from official brand resources).
