About the Club

360° Toastmasters Club Bengaluru is a community built around growth—where feedback is kind, learning is constant, and every meeting is designed to help you become a better communicator and a stronger leader. We were chartered on 1 September 2025.

Supportive Culture

New or experienced, you’ll feel welcomed and guided from day one.

Structured Growth

Prepared speeches, evaluations, and Table Topics—practice that actually sticks.

Leadership in Action

Learn to lead meetings—and yourself—confidently and calmly.
